My name is Ahoh Kwamena Eshan(Mr) the Head of Depart (HOD) of welding department Winneba Technical Vocational Training Institude.I am an Advanced weilding craft practice holder and I manage this department with Mr Issac Naa Donkoh(National craftmanship certificate holder).

Teaching Basics are we teach theory and practicals, that is fourty percent(40%) on trade theory

  • Trade Theory
  • Science and Calculation
  • Bench fitting
  • Black Smithing
And We take sixty(60%) percent on trade Technology including:
  • Arc Weldind Practicals
  • Gas welding
  • Bench Fitting
  • Black Smitting Practice
  • Frabrication

The course Range between two and half years for tutering and six month for industrial training(O.J.T). The department take external contract from clients because it helps generate income for the department and also helps the trainees to get more knowledge on what they are being trained for.

Trainees are allowed to take interest in other trade Courses such as Mechnical Engineering Machining Plumbing Gas fitting because most of these theory and practicals are offered in this department

Welding is an essential component of many industries without welding many forms of metal works cannot be achieve example Vehicle, Aircraft, Ships, Submarine and Sky buildings. After completing of course some trainees are able to established themselves, others also work in factories.
