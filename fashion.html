Mildred Esi Affren, the head of department WINNTECH, professional teacher and certified in Higher textiles studies and Bachelor of Art, Fashion and Textiles studies and Bachelor of Art, Fashion and textiles education from accredited institution of Takoradi Technical University and University of education Winneba.

She is expert in both the theory and the practical in the field of Fashion, Pattern drafting, garment construction(both tex) and millinery accessories. She is being assisted with qualified teachers:

  • Mary Bedu Twumasi who is a graduate of BlueCrest University with Bsc. FAstion design with speciality in:

    • Pattern draft
    • Garment construction
    • Fashion Illustration

    as well certified with Competency Base Training(C.B.T) from COTVET.

  • Louisa Clement also a graduate from University Of Education, Winneba. She is expect in both Ladies and men's wear as well as kids wear.

  • Betty Arthur who is also a graduate of Accra Technical University with honored in HND spacialised in Bridal wears and Casual Wears

Teaching Bases of the department is made up of 70% practical and 30% theory. Some tools required by students of the fashion department:

  • french curve
  • pinking shears
  • tracing wheel
  • timble
  • pliers
  • pins
  • dummy
  • scissors
  • sewing machines
  • seam ripper
  • tape measure
  • cutters
  • needles
  • head blocks
Most of these tools and equiments will be made available to students by the school.

Students get the opportunity of partaking and organising a termly Fashion show for the trainees to enable them show off their skills and talents. Also Students go on attachments during vacation for the same purpose as the above. Also students are always involved when ever there is an external contract for the department.

Subject based practicals that we focus much on are:

  • Pattern Drafing
  • Garment Construction
  • Millinery

Student are encouraged to partake course like ICT since the basic knowledge is this course would go a long way to become better Designers and promote their brands using the various technological tools made available.
