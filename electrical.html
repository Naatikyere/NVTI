Teaching bases on both practical and theory are seventy percentage(70%) and thirty percentage(30%) respectivitely.The department has trained lot of trainees and most of them are employed and others are self employed. The department had trained the trainees to be good at wiring a complete house and also repairing of domestic appliances.

Students do go on attachments during the end of their programme to help them apply the skills they have aquired over their stay in the department to the outside world. In addition, the department takes external contracts and students are encouraged to partake in these activities since its a way to help them sharpen their skills.

Students are actually allowed to take interest in other courses Expecially ICT in most of our work revolve around the use of ICT tools and also the basic knowledge and understanding will help students to be better at what they do.
