message from The Manager

Hello and welcome to Winneba Technical Institude, Once called Winneba Vocational Training Institude which was chanced from the service of GES to Ghana TVET Service. We entreat that students who are privilaged to be enrolled under the Ghana TVET Service should know that the certificates that are awarded upon completion is higher than that of the vocational training institudes. So students are encouraged to study well since that puts them on a higher value in the job market.

The school has been equipped with Ultra-modern tools and equipments to be used in making teaching and learning go as smoothly as possible and also enjoyable for maximum understanding of the various courses.

The Institution is aimed at producing highly refined individuals with skill set who would be a part of the nation building to make Ghana great.

We encourage you all to come to Winneba Technical Institude and learn a trade or sharpen your skills. Thank You!!!!
